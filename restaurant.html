Restaurants of the Nothern Quarter

The Northern Quarter is home to many independent restaurants. Each have a specific theme and unique atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a casual night out or an expensive treat, you can find anything here. The variety of cuisines provided will keep you coming back to try more. Each one worth the trip. Here we have provided our favourites, but we urge you to go out, exlore taste and enjoy for yourself.

Yard & Coop



Yard and Coop is the ‘home of buttermilk fried chicken’ found in the heart of Northern Quarter, 37 Edge Street. They serve up a great range of the classic chicken shop style of foods in a modern and professional restaurant style manner.
The interior of the restaurant is a mix between wood and metal for the tables, chairs and a stock room style caged off area near the entrance which gives the restaurant an industrial feel. There are also big wide windows that let in light making the main eating areas feel spacious and airy. The staff are very friendly and well informed on the menu making a visit to Yard and Coop a friendly and enjoyable experience.

Turtle Bay



Turtle Bay brings a different type of venue to the northern quarter. A taste of paradise with its Inspiration of Caribbean island beach stack and street vendor food. With a great tasty selection of food and drinks, from beach burgers to BBQ jerk chicken and a range of salads there is something for everyone here.
