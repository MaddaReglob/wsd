Street Art

Manchester's Northern Quarter is famous for its various street art designs found throughout the area. Some are visibly displayed on more prominent buildings whist others are hidden away like treasures begging to be discovered. Whether you are just passing through, or actively searching for these gems, these pieces are worth the time. They are truly wonderous to behold. In this section, we have included a selection of our favourite pieces. They can give you an idea of the beauties that you can find simply by walking the streets of Manchester.

Giant Blue Tit


That's right, you read it correctly, a Giant Blue Tit. This piece of art is one of our favourites because of the sheer size and detail, a true master piece of work. This artwork was commissoned by the clothing brand Converse and painted by the artist Faunographic. The Giant Blue Tit can be found on Newton Street.

Giant Blue Tit

Jeweled Woman

Four artists from Subism collective created this masterful piece of artwork on the side of the ‘Ride Low ’ bike shop in 2011. Funded by Red Bull, this artwork took five days to complete. This piece is referred to as a lot of different names including ‘the wall’ ‘sixxis’ but we called it the ‘jewled woman’.

Jeweled Woman

Manchester Bees

This piece truly shows off manchester in all it's glory taking the iconic bee and displaying it as a centerpiece of a work of art. This demonstrates what it really means to be a part of Manchester. It really captures the heart of it all from the location being at the heart of it, to the imagery. Giving you a sense of pride and love for the place it resides.

Manchester Bees
