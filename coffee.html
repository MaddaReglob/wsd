The Koffee pot has been a go to for many manchunicans for over a decade, since its opening in 2007, with fantastic vegan options, super fast service and super friendly waiting staff, the Koffee pot if great for both its coffee and food alike.. The koffee pot offers free WiFi, Vegan options, super fast food service and friendly waiting staff. Basically everything you come to expect from the northern quarter. If it's the vibe you are after, then look no further.

Chapter One




Chapter One is a quirky independently owned coffee and book shop found on Lever Street, only a short walk from Piccadilly gardens. Serving some of the best locally roasted coffee in Northern Quarter, Chapter One is a great place to come and chill out, read or even get on with some work. They also offer free fast Wi-Fi for customers. A good range of books are also on display around the shop which customers are free to pick up and read.
